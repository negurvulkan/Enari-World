## Short profile

The Veyrath Republic is a delegative council republic with a strong procedural ethos. Its official long form is `Veyrath-naar thalor-jorath`; `thalor-jorath`, `enar-jorath` and `Veyrath-naar` mark different sides of the same state idea.

Politically, they are united by broad participation, a strong archive and legal culture, functional administration and a consciously anti-monarchical symbolic language. Territorially, the state is divided into `lonath-jorath` associations and flexibly tailored regional `jorath` provinces instead of rigid hereditary lands.

## Canon in short form

- The Veyrath Republic is a delegative council republic with a strong procedural ethos.
- Its central organ is the `Isyr-thalor`, embedded in archives, law and public consultation.
- Territorially, the state is divided into `lonath-jorath` associations and regional `jorath` provinces.
- The capital **Thalor-talen** is a polycentric council and water town with several functional cores.
- The central council building is called **Torim-kovath** and is deliberately anti-monarchical, open and fivefold symbolized.
- The state has anti-monarchical symbols such as the open five-fold shape, empty middle, delegation ribbons and the guiding formula `Vel. Torim. Therin. Jorin. Ravin.`
- Political currents organize themselves more as lines, networks and council blocs than as rigid mass parties.
- The republic remains internally characterized by tensions between openness, order, security and social equality.
