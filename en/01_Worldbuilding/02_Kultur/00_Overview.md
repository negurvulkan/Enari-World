# Culture - Overview

Purpose: Collector's folder for the cultural, social and institutional foundations of the Enari outside of the pure linguistic description.

## Content

- [01_Kinderhäuser.md](./01_Kinderhaeuser.md) as a basic text on the function, structure, personnel structure, social character and register role of Enarian children's homes
- [02_Kastensystem.md](./02_Kastensystem.md) on the social position of Enu and the status paradox of biologically central but institutionally often lower valued roles
- [03_Erziehung.md](./03_Erziehung.md) as a place for training and socialization models
- [04_Militär.md](./04_Militaer.md) as a place for service culture, hierarchies and physical discipline
- [05_Rituale_und_Alltagskultur.md](./05_Rituale_und_Alltagskultur.md) on visibility rules, clothing codes, social readability and the cultural use of clusters and markers
- [06_Veyrathische_Republik/00_Übersicht.md](./06_Veyrathische_Republik/00_Overview.md) on the republican state order, capital structure, council architecture, administrative logic and the political currents of the Veyrath Republic
- [07_Veytharische_Krone/00_Übersicht.md](./07_Veytharische_Krone/00_Overview.md) on the caste- and office-based monarchy, its order of elevation, court structure, symbolism and the ideological counter-model to the republic
