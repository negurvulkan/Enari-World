# Enu in the military

Despite their often low status, Enu are not limited to nursing professions. In many countries they form a significant part of the ordinary military infantry, medical services, logistics and other endurance-oriented activities.

![[../99_Medien/03_Fotos_und_Portraits/01_Enu/enu__foto__sport__foto__v01.png|caption=Sporty reference view of an Enu, suitable for roles with high endurance and resilience.|medium|right|popover]]

## Suitability profile

Their physical robustness, stability and muscular endurance make Enu suitable for long-term stress. They are therefore particularly suitable for:

- Marching and carrying services
- Replenishment and logistics
- Caring for the wounded
- Field medical and security tasks

## Cultural tension

The inclusion of the Enu in military or physically demanding roles is often in tension with their image as a nurturing and nurturing gender. It is precisely this dual role of care and resilience that shapes their cultural perception in many regions.
