# Veytharisch - Lexicon

## Lexical guidelines
- Verbs appear in the lexicon as infinitives `-en`.
- Many basic words are clearly related to Veyrathi, but shifted phonologically and grammatically.
- Vocabulary favors dense syllables, hard word endings and reduced final vowels.
- Legal, council and seafaring vocabulary preserves particularly many old cognates.
