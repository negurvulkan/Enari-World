# Languages - Overview

Purpose: Collection area for the historical, cultural and functional languages of the Enarian world.

## Main modules

- [01_Ur-Veyatisch](./01_Ur-Veyatisch/00_Overview.md): reconstructed or early developed preliminary stage of the later Veyatisch language development
- [02_Alt-Veyata](./02_Alt-Veyata/00_Overview.md): common late intermediate before the differentiation of younger varieties
- [03_Veyrathi](./03_Veyrathi/00_Overview.md): common lingua franca with extensive expansion
- [04_Veytharisch](./04_Veytharisch/00_Overview.md): independent daughter or sister development with its own theory of sounds and forms
- [05_Saithanisch](./05_Saithanisch/00_Overview.md): old standard, scholarly and archival language outside the Veyatic language family

## Additional material

- [06_Vergleich_vier_veyatischer_Sprachen.md](./06_Vergleich_vier_veyatischer_Sprachen.md): Comparison of central development lines and profiles

## Note

The language area combines language history, grammar, lexis, registers and culture-related technical language. The level of expansion of the individual language modules varies; Veyrathi and Saithanic are currently particularly well documented.

A new astronomy language level has also been added: [Veyrathi - Astronomy and Space Terminology](./03_Veyrathi/19_Astronomie_und_Raumfahrtterminologie.md) for standard naming, [Saithan - Astronomy and Astrophysics](./05_Saithanisch/11_Astronomie_und_Astrophysik.md) for learned specialist prose, [Veyrathi - Name and Style Sheet of the Iyath-Systems](./03_Veyrathi/20_Namens_und_Stilblatt_des_Iyath-Systems.md) for proper names and glossations and [Veyrathi - Register and use of system names](./03_Veyrathi/21_Register_und_Gebrauch_der_Systemnamen.md) for in-universe register distribution.

A botanical language level has now been added for Naar: [Veyrathi - Plant Register Naars](./03_Veyrathi/22_Pflanzenregister_Naars.md) collects the large everyday and world-related plant vocabulary, while [Saithan - Botanical Taxonomy and Plant Systematics](./05_Saithanisch/12_Botanische_Taxonomie_und_Pflanzensystematik.md) provides the taught classification.

This is now supplemented by a faunistic language level: [Veyrathi - Fauna Register Naars](./03_Veyrathi/23_Faunaregister_Naars.md) contains typical animal shapes, name building blocks and everyday names of the Naars animal world.
