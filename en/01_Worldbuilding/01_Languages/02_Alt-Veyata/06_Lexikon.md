# Old Veyata - Lexicon

## Guiding principle
- The lexicon shows Old Veyata as an independent intermediate stage, not just as "early Veyrathi".
- Many forms are clearly related to the daughter languages, but still retain more open full forms or older fields of meaning.
- The tables deliberately mention the later reflexes in Veyrathi and Veytharisch so that the intermediate position remains visible.

## Overall lexical impression
- Alt-Veyata already prefers open, regular trunk exits without completely giving up the full old depth.
- In the vocabulary it is clearly visible that Veyrathi usually continues the more open core and Veytharisch often continues the denser edges.
- This is precisely why Alt-Veyata's lexicon is particularly informative about the family's historical middle position.
