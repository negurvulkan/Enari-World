# Ur-Veyatic - Overview

Purpose: Reconstructed common origin language of Veyrathi and Veythar.

##Status
- Ur-Veyatic is not a modern national language or a current standard language.
- It acts as an early language stage of the old Enaris coastal, river and council alliances: cultic, legal, seafaring and epic, not nation-state.
- The language should sound old, credible and independent: heavier and deeper than Veyrathi, more open and resonant than Veythar.
- In terms of world feeling, Ur-Veyatisch consciously stands at a distance from today's centers of power. It is ancestral speech, not official norm.

## Overall profile
- Sound: sonorous, archaic, slightly mineral; common sequences like `ae`, `ey`, `au`, `th`, `zh`, `-r`, `-th`, `-n`
- Typology: moderately suffixing with old relic particles; Roots remain clearly visible
- Sentence structure: strongly structured with information; Legal and table style favors verb late position, narrative style allows early verb
- Noun phrase: basic pattern `Det + Nomen + Adjektiv`; Prefixed adjectives are solemn, contrastive or oath-like
- Verbs: many basic forms end in `-ar`; old particles for prospective, irrealis and negation come before the verb
- Morphological markers: abstracts on `-at`, animate/socially marked plurals on `-i`, collective plurals on `-an`

## Role in the language family
- Veyrathi inherited from Ur-Veyatic the more open syllable guidance, the tendency towards clear stems and the copulaless normal predication.
- Veytharisch inherited the denser word margins, the stronger brackets in the sentence and the more conservative preservation of old particles.
- Ur-Veyatic is therefore not an average between the two daughter languages, but rather the credible older source from which both have preserved different parts.
