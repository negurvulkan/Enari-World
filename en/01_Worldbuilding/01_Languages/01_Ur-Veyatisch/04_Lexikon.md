# Ur-Veyatic - core lexicon

## Guiding principle
- The lexicon shows the common old root layer of the language family.
- Forms are intentionally neither modern nor earthly transparent; they follow the internal logic of Veyrathi and Veytharisch.
- The Ur-Veatic entries are not just back translations, but the older source behind the well-known cognates.
