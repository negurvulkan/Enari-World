# Saithanic - Enu technical terms

## Purpose

This file converts the `A` priorities from `08_Enu_Terminbank.md` into concrete Saithan technical terms. The focus is on a medical-biological register for anatomy, physiology, reproductive biology, developmental biology and evolutionary biology of the Enu.

Specialized replacement forms for Latin or Latinized anatomy terms of the Enu register are continued in `10_Enu_Klassische_Terminologie.md`.

## Working principles
- Where possible, existing Saithan roots from `05_Wurzelregister.md` and `06_Fachserien.md` are reused.
- Where necessary, this file introduces **local additional roots** specifically needed for Enu biology.
- The new terms should appear like classic scholarly composites: dense, right-wing and productively developable.
- This file sets **working canon**, not absolute immutable finality. Later standardization remains possible.

## Quick note for use in Enu article
- In continuous texts, the anatomical, reproductive biology and endocrinological core terms in particular should be replaced.
- Descriptive adjectives such as `breit`, `weich`, `kraeftig` do not need to be Saithanized.
- Terms like `mauthglarth`, `vargstaurum`, `harenthskor`, `nirmthesketh` and `velthklaurum` are particularly suitable as later loanword sources for Veyrathi and Veytharisch.
