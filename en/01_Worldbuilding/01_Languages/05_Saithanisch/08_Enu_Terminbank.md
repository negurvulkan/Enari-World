# Saithan - Enu term bank

## Purpose

This file collects the most important terms from the article on Enu biology, which can later be usefully replaced by Saithan technical terms. **No final Saithan forms are determined here**. Instead, a prioritized work list is created for later appointment creation.

Status:
- The `A` priorities have now been specifically worked out in `09_Enu_Fachtermini.md`.
- This file remains the conceptual preselection and prioritization.

## Working logic
- Everyday descriptions can remain in German.
- Anatomical, physiological, reproductive biology, developmental biology and evolutionary biology terms are the best candidates for Saithan terminology.
- The `Vermutliche Serie` column only refers to the later subject family, not to a finished term.

## Priority key
- `A` = should most likely become Saithan in medical, biological or academic texts
- `B` = good specialist candidate, but not necessarily for every use
- `C` = optional; more for high register, glossaries or later specialization
