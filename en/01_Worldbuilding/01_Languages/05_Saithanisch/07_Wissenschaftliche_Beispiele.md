# Saithan - Scientific Examples

## Register notes
- The following examples are in a strictly formal teaching and commenting tone.
- Formulaic speaking prefers copula, equation and causal derivation.
- Symbolic notation can be directly combined with Saithan terms.

## Standard formulas
- `Tharneth = grath x vekthveimeth.` = Force = mass times acceleration.
- `Torveth = grath / uthar.` = Density = Mass per volume of space.
- `Neskorveth = 1 / neskir.` = Frequency = one per unit of time.
- `Khaurveimeth norvek khaurkaiveth.` = Temperature change results in the heat measurement.
- `Shaurvailketh neskorveth norvek.` = The light wave determines the clock ratio.
