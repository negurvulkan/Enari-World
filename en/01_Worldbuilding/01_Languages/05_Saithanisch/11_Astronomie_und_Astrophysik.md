# Saithanic - Astronomy and Astrophysics

## Purpose

This file collects **Saitan high register terms** for observation, orbital mechanics, planetological analysis and astroclimatic description. The focus is not on everyday names of individual celestial bodies, but on the terms with which observatories, archives, educational institutions and systematic research work.

## Working principles

- Where possible, existing roots from `05_Wurzelregister.md` and the series from `06_Fachserien.md` will continue to be used.
- Proper names such as **Iyath**, **Saivor**, **Naar**, **Seyra** and **Orun** remain as fixed identifiers in Saithan technical prose; Above all, **measurement, classification, field and process terms** are saithanized.
- Saithan astronomy is strongly **methodological**: observation, measurement, cataloging, orbital law and environmental classification are more important than poetic object names.
- New forms of this file are composites of existing scientific roots and represent **working canon** for taught texts.

In current professional prose, the following hybrid form is particularly plausible:

- `Iyathen shaurkaiveth` for photometric data of the main star Iyath
- `Iyathen shaurskaiveth` for classification and star profile of the main star
- `Saivoren krothveimeth` for perturbation or orbit change values of the companion star Saivor
- `Saivoren shaurskaiveth` for the learned classification of the companion star
- `Naaren oarvkaiveth` for homeworld planetological measurement data
- `Seyraen krothkaiveth` for orbit measurements of the large main moon
- `Orunen krothkaiveth` for orbit data of the smaller companion moon
- `Tirashen grathtorveth` for density and body data of the inner rocky planet
- `Kalethen neldkhesmeth` for atmosphere matrix and gas composition from Kaleth
- `Kalethen draemtorveth` for pressure distributions on the greenhouse planet
- `Esyren draemkaiveth` for cold colony planet pressure measurements
- `Veyronen neldkhesmeth` for the analysis of the gaseous shells of the gas giant
- `Thalosen krothskorn` for ring and orbital systems of the outer ice giant
- `Zeythen krothmnorum` for cataloged asteroid belt data

## Register note

Saithanic does not replace every concrete name in astronomical texts. For names, map labels and popular explanatory prose, **Veyrathi forms** often remain more readable. Saithanic gains its weight where it is defined, measured, classified and recorded.

The standard counterpart to this is [Veyrathi - Astronomy and Space Terminology](../03_Veyrathi/19_Astronomie_und_Raumfahrtterminologie.md). For proper names, choice of style and scholarly name glosses see also [Veyrathi - Name and Style Sheet of the Iyath System](../03_Veyrathi/20_Namens_und_Stilblatt_des_Iyath-Systems.md).
