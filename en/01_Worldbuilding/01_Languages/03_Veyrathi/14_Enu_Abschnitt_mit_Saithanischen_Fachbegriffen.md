# Veyrathi - Enu section with Saithan terminology

## Purpose

This file is a **free technical translation** of the most recently developed Enu section into Veyrathi. The sentence structure and everyday grammar remain Veyrathian, while the main terms taught are deliberately replaced by **Saithian specialist forms**.

## Register principle
- Veyrathi carries sentence structure, particles and everyday verbs.
- Saithanic provides the high level medical and archival terminology.
- The Saithan technical terms remain here intentionally **unsmoothed**, i.e. more like quoted high-register terms than like fully fitted everyday inherited words.
- Existing native Veyrathi terms such as `pethbulen`, `usharbulen` or `pethsuren` are intentionally **not** used in this version.

## Translation

### Harenthskor

Ar harenthskor enu'ren veza ar mauthglarthsaiveth va ar ravath. Harenthim or thaelglarth niva ar mauthglarthim va ar mauthdraskthrum. Ar mauthharenthum jora ar ravath. Ar veltheth nari. Ar darun-dirath len. Dar ar darun-varath, tharnharenthim va daurnharenthim dera se.

### Särn-taur va Vargthrumim

Ar murnvargskor enu'ren tura ar nukarthvenkskorn. Ar nukarthvenkselkskelthim ve tura jorin va selai, va se veda ar murnvargthrumskor len. Ar ärvmauthglarthim tura oranges: ar mauthglarthmnaurim shyul ar sarel shi. Se jora ar glarthdrethim nari va ar mauthdraskthrum. Oranen duvarmurndrethmnaurim tura. Se jora ar murndraskthrum. Ar skarnmnaurbrimin tura naror va ar winath draz, sava ar enari särnim vuni.

### Vargthrum Draz

Ar mauthdraskthrum tura ar murnvargthrumskor jorin draz. Se heva mauthstaursaiv vuni. Ar mauthstaursaiv fera savar va korenlar draz. Se heva mirisar, yaran va relen. Harenthim or thaelglarth va or särnvargthrumim yava ar glarthsaiveth, va ar mauthstaursaiv hena ar mauthglarthim va uthtala ar mauthglarthmnaurim. Ar harenthneskvailketh no tura. Ar glarthsaiveth hena vath varath va ravath.

### Haurnbrimselthum va Oranen Ravath

Ar haurnbrimselthum yava a mauthglarthim draz. Sena mauthglarthsaiveth nava pethan ar jankap-kylar oranges. Enu heva vargselthum ve. Sena, ar mauthstaursaiv bava ar jankap va ar jankap-dovar. Oranges, ar mauthglarthsaiv nava pethan ar jankap-kylar va jora ar ravath oranges.

### MauthstaursaivAr mauthstaursaiv fera ar staur-saiv enu'ren sa savar va korenlar draz. Se bava ar jankap-staur, heva ar jankap-dovar zorin, va nava thavar ar enu-kylajan. Ar mauthstaursaiv draz va silen-thunen. Ar zeya haurn enari, se hena vuni va heva ar enu-kylajan ar yaran ärv. Senai, ar janakor hura ar mauthstaursaiv vuni va heva se oranen, ena ar jannavath or sen särnim hena.

### Oranges ar Jankap

Oranen ar jankap hena, enu niva ar ravath jorin. Sena mauthglarthim niva ar mauthglarthsaiv va ar zeya-kylar hena ar pethan savir. Ar ravath oranges fera draz jorin.

## German translation

### Endocrine system

The Enu endocrine system is focused on mammary gland secretion and nursing. Hormones from the pituitary gland control the mammary glands and the nutritive storage bladder. The nursing and lactation hormone complex ties supply to behavior. The metabolism remains calm. The tendency to aggression is low. However, during conflict or military service, activation and stress hormones work against this.

### Sexual form and reproductive organs

The urogenital tract of the Enu is located in the ventral pelvic area. Two pubic skin folds lie on the sides and partially cover the urogenital complex. Below are the external nutritive glands: nine gland openings in three transverse rows. These are connected to the nutritive storage bladder via short ducts. Below this are the double urethral openings. They lead to the urinary bladder. The terminal excretory opening is located dorsally and is clearly separated from the other organs.

### Internal organ structure

Inside lies the nutritive storage bladder as the central organ of the urogenital complex. It contains the nutritive matrix fluid. This liquid is rich in lipids and proteins. It also contains polysaccharides, water and electrolytes. Hormones from the pituitary gland and the sexual organs trigger secretion, and the matrix fluid flows from there into the mammary glands and outwards to the gland openings. There is no regular hormonal cycle. Secretions are released as needed and depending on the supply situation.

### Maturation and subsequent care

With the threshold of maturity, the mammary glands grow significantly. Enu has two reproductive functional areas. On the one hand, the nutritive matrix fluid prepares the spawning capsule and provides the young animal with early energy. On the other hand, the mammary gland secretion later nourishes the newly hatched young animal and ties this supply to care.

### Matrix fluid

The nutritive matrix fluid is a dense, substance-rich base fluid of the Enu. It forms the basic matrix of the spawning capsule, keeps its shell stable and provides energy for early embryonic development. The liquid is dense and milky-brown. In the early development of the Enari, it was formed in large quantities and used in open water spaces. Today, breeding centers collect this fluid in large quantities and hold it ready until the reproductive contributions of the other sexes are added.

### After hatching

After hatching, enu take over the care together. Then the mammary glands release their secretion, and the young child receives easily digestible nutrition from it. This subsequent care remains strongly organized on a communal basis.

## Quick note
- This version is intentionally **high register** and seems more scholarly than normal Veyrathi.
- In everyday or popular jargon, Veyrathi would rather use the already existing native forms such as `pethbulen`, `usharbulen`, `pethsuren` and `pethglaren`.
- For later canonization, you can decide whether this file remains as a pure style sample or serves as the beginning of a real Saithanized medicine register for Veyrathi.
