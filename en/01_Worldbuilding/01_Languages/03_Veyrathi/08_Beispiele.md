# Veyrathi - Examples

Note: The IPA lines reflect the broad teaching and standard pronunciation with word accent; Everyday language allophones (e.g. `th` -> `[tŐ™]/[t]`) are not specifically noted here.

## Basic sets
- Well, now. = I speak.
  IPA: [nu ňąvej.…ĺa]
- Nu veyran. = I spoke.
  IPA: [nu ňąvej.…ĺan]
- Nu veyrara. = I will speak.
  IPA: [nu vejňą…ĺa.…ĺa]

## Paradigm block (morphology)
1. Well, now. = I speak.
   IPA: [nu ňąvej.…ĺa]
2. Well no veyra. = I don't speak.
   IPA: [nu no ňąvej.…ĺa]
3. Nu sa veyra. = I am speaking.
   IPA: [nu sa ňąvej.…ĺa]
4. Nu han veyran. = I have spoken (completed).
   IPA: [nu han ňąvej.…ĺan]
5. Nu veyrara. = I will speak.
   IPA: [nu vejňą…ĺa.…ĺa]
6. Veyra! = Speak!
   IPA: [ňąvej.…ĺa]
7. No veyra! = Don't speak!
   IPA: [no ňąvej.…ĺa]
8. Nu kei veyra, se veyra. = If I were to speak, the other person would speak.
   IPA: [nu ňąke.i ňąvej.…ĺa, se ňąvej.…ĺa]
9. Nor seyra ser. = The Enor sees himself.
   IPA: [no…ĺ ňąsej.…ĺa se…ĺ]
10. Nu'ren naar is draz. = My homeworld is very strong/powerful.
    IPA: [ňąnu Ē.…ĺen ňąna.a…ĺ is d…ĺaz]
11. Se'ren thalor zeya. = Their advice is new.
    IPA: [ňąse Ē.…ĺen ňąőła.lo…ĺ ňąze.ja]
12. Ar nor len draz. = The Enor is rather strong.
    IPA: [a…ĺ no…ĺ len d…ĺaz]
13. Ar nor is draz. = The Enor is very strong.
    IPA: [a…ĺ no…ĺ is d…ĺaz]

    IPA: [a…ĺ ňąvej.…ĺaőł ňąze.ja]
15. Ar veyror veyra. = The speaker speaks.
    IPA: [a…ĺ ňąvej.…ĺo…ĺ ňąvej.…ĺa]

## Interlinear glosses
Legend: 1EN = 1st person Enu, 3ENOR = 3rd person Enor, NEUT = neutral, POSS = possessive, DEF = definite,
NEG = Negation, PROG = Progression Aspect, RES = Resultative, PST = Past, FUT = Future,
INTS = intensifier, DIM = attenuator, Q = interrogative particle, REFL = reflexive, COMP = complementizer, CAUS = causal, CONC = concessive, COND.R = realis-conditional, COND.I = irrealis-conditional,
COP = supporting verb of the marked predication, WH = interrogative form, VOC = vocative, HES = hesitation particle, ATTN = attention control, HOLD = hold particle, CHAL = doubt particle, FRAG = fragment pull.

- Velan nu veyra. = I speak clearly. Contrast focus on `velan`.
  IPA: [ňąve.lan nu ňąvej.…ĺa]
