# Avar Janath

## Note on names
The names `Avar`, `Eran`, `Veth`, `Syra`, `Thaal`, `Ilyr` are treated as proto-Veyrath proper names.
`Syra` and `Thaal` are intentionally slightly adapted so that they read clearly as ancient names rather than modern root forms.

## Veyrathi version

### Ar Janath va Avar

Sena yavath, no sirath.
No moran.
No vayen.

Va ar yavath, eko Avar.
Avar jorin, demir, va goven enar.
Avar no zorin va no darun.
Avar vesar goven, vesar jera, va no tala.

Va ar shalen, tenar yava.

From art tenar, ar fira umir zera.
Ar umir no kera irath ren.
Ar umir no kova, ar umir eka jana.
Ar umir dera Avar goven, vath ar umir lenor.

Ar umir dera Avar.
No va kerun.
Va kiren.

Ar umir jana ar Avar-sein va fira kiren draz yava.
Avar no drun-janen, ar no korven; Avar janen va nirath.

### Ar Fira Framing

From eko kiren, eko savel yava:

Eran - ar felan-draz.
Pavar, thavar, talath.
From Eran, ar zyran yava.

Veth - ar doren-mora.
Lurim, moran, melyth.
Veth drava ar wevar va moran-jorath.

Syra - ar lavin.
Korven va janath, lirun, selor.
From Syra, ar yoron va haloren yava.

Thaal - ar thuvor.
Struk, meral, koren.
Thaal kova ar dusar va naarn.

Ilyr - ar ilyan.
Jorin, jiva, demath-irath.
Ilyr fera from nulkorven ar demir enar.

Ar umir wina, vath ar umir therath ya.
Avar no vesar goven.

### Ar Yavath va Moran-Jorath

Ar fira virel jora no; ar wina ren va ren.

Eran sira ar na zyran.
Veth mora vesar.
Syra kelir va duma.
Thaal kima va naar-koren.
Ilyr lora.

Yar ar fira virel jera va eko tiken, ar jiva-naarn yava.

### Ar Yavath va Naar

Naar no zavin naarn.

Va eko zeya zyran-jorath, virel va Thaal zhera.
Eran pava ar koren ren.
Syra hura va yoron.
Veth heva jorin.

Dar yar eko draz-splen va Ilyr felan va haloren, zeya yavath yava va Naar.

From mineral-karuv, ar na kylen yava.
From kylen, wevar.
From wevar, jyren.

Va oranges - far sarinir - ar Enari yava.
