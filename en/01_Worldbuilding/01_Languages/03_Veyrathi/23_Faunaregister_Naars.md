# Veyrathi - Naars fauna register

## Purpose

This module collects the Veyrathian animal vocabulary for **Naar**. It lists everyday animal names, typical name building blocks and concrete example species for coasts, swamps, shallow water, wet forests and edge zones.

Status of this version:

- **8 productive animal base forms**
- **28 elaborate animal names**
- Focus on wading and mud fauna, swimmers, shoals, gliders, grazers and predators of Naar

## Guiding principles

- The names remain **veyrathic**, manageable and usable for everyday life, hunting, animal husbandry and trade.
- Compounds are **right-headed**: The last term names the animal shape, the first term describes color, habitat, food, substance or behavior.
- Habitat members such as `feyan`, `norel`, `senel`, `noren`, `doren`, `meral`, `selun` and `varun` remain highly productive.
- Material and functional links such as `bulor`, `sirath`, `pethan`, `sulen`, `melin`, `virim`, `halim`, `thunim`, `tovim` or `silim` specify protective film, glow, nutrition, fruit binding or coloring.

## Enarian usage

The Enari use Naar animals primarily along relationships typical of wet areas:

- **Stance:** `thunim-selun-gauren`, `pethan-doren-gauren`, `noren-lavin-gauren`
- **Basin and riparian economy:** `melin-doren-nasken`, `pethan-norel-dralen`, `sirath-feyan-vakel`
- **Swarm Usage:** `sirath-brulen`, `pethan-brulen`, `renath-selun-brulen`
- **Dangerous species:** `norel-thoren`, `feyan-thoren`, `meral-thoren`
- **Symbol and Omen Fauna:** `sirath-meral-selkar`, `silim-feyan-dralen`, `bulor-varun-selkar`
