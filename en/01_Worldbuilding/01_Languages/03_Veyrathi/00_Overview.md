# Veyrathi - Overview

Purpose: Lingua franca of most nations in Enari.

## Content
- Status and distribution
- Registers and varieties
- Level of standardization
- Historical preliminary stage: Old Veyata as a common late stage after the Ur-Veyatic and before the separation into Veyrathian and Veytharian
- Storage: separate folder `Alt-Veyata` with the complete series of modules from `00_Overview.md` to `10_Zusammenfassung.md`
- Additional module: `14_Enu_Abschnitt_mit_Saithanischen_Fachbegriffen.md` as a free Veyrathi specialist translation with Saithan high register
- Additional module: `15_Enu_Abschnitt_einfach_mit_Saithan_Glossen.md` as a layman's version with Veyrathi base and Saithan bracket shapes
- Additional module: `16_Namensregister.md` as a register for child names, adult nicknames, meanings, origins and variants
- Additional module: `17_Ortsnamen.md` as a toponymy module for place names, landscape names, historical layers and regional variants
- Additional module: `18_Kinderhausnamen.md` as a system for `janakor` names, short forms and origin markers in registers
- Additional module: `19_Astronomie_und_Raumfahrtterminologie.md` as standard terminology for star systems, moons, planetology and everyday space travel
- Additional module: `20_Namens_und_Stilblatt_des_Iyath-Systems.md` as a name and style sheet for star, moon and planet names with Saithan specialist glosses
- Additional module: `21_Register_und_Gebrauch_der_Systemnamen.md` as an in-universe profile for school, observatory, administrative and everyday use of the same system names
- Additional module: `22_Pflanzenregister_Naars.md` as a large botanical vocabulary for Naar with 40 families and 320 named plant forms
- Additional module: `23_Faunaregister_Naars.md` as an animal vocabulary for Naar with productive animal forms and concrete fauna names
