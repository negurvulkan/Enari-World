# Astraea

Astraea is a cold oceanic world with broad continental shelf seas and luminous reef trenches.

Its best known inhabitants are the [[01_Species_Lysari.md|Lysari]], whose floating cities orbit the warmer current bands near the equatorial fracture lines.
