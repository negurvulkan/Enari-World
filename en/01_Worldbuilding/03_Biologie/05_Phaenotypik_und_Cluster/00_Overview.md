# Enari phenotypics and clusters

This section describes the visible variation of the Enari. The focus is not on ancestry myths or rigid “races”, but rather on a specialist system of cultural groups, phenotype clusters and individual markers.

## Leading distinction

The Enarian administration summarizes the basic rule in a famous mnemonic:

**Ethnicity = culture. Clusters = appearance. Markers = building blocks.**

![[../../99_Medien/07_Phaenotypik_und_Cluster/01_Allgemein/enari__gruppe__phaenotypcluster_auswahl_ganzkoerper__beschriftet__grafik__v01.png|caption=Labeled comparison view of several Enari phenotype clusters.|large|center|popover]]

This distinguishes between social affiliation, statistically common appearance profiles and concrete observable characteristics.
