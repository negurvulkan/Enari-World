## Function in the project

The systems described here provide the basic model of the enarian organism. Gender-specific deviations and specializations are then further differentiated under [04_Genders](../04_Geschlechter/00_Overview.md).

[06_Haut_und_Sinnesorgane.md](./06_Haut_und_Sinnesorgane.md) is particularly important for describing external variation. This page forms the anatomical basis for the later [Marker and Cluster System](../05_Phaenotypik_und_Cluster/00_Overview.md).
