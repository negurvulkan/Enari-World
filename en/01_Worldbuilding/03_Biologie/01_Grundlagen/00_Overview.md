# Basics of Enari biology

This section collects overarching biological starting points for the species: species profile, evolution, ecology and general physiological relationships. The evolution, ecology and development logic of the Enari is now designed as a coherent framework.

## Documents

- [01_Artprofil_Enari.md](./01_Artprofil_Enari.md): Basic profile of the species with body structure, marker logic and biological classification.
- [02_Evolution.md](./02_Evolution.md): Hypothesis on the gradual emergence of the five-sex reproductive system.
- [03_Ökologie.md](./03_Oekologie.md): ecological basis of the species on Naar with links to the Iyath system.
- [04_Physiologie_Grundlagen.md](./04_Physiologie_Grundlagen.md): general physiological developmental axes of growth, maturation and aging.
- [05_Flora_Fauna_und_Lebensräume_Naars.md](./05_Flora_Fauna_und_Lebensraeume_Naars.md): Biosphere profile of Naar with dominant flora and fauna types and 28 typical habitats.
- [06_Fauna_Naars.md](./06_Fauna_Naars.md): more detailed dossier on the wildlife of Naar with key types, example species and enarian usage.
- [07_Leitorganismen_Nahrungsketten_und_Gürtel_Naars.md](./07_Leitorganismen_Nahrungsketten_und_Guertel_Naars.md): Condensation of the biosphere into iconic lead organisms, central food chains and planetary belts.
- [08_Feldhandbuch_Leitorganismen_Naars.md](./08_Feldhandbuch_Leitorganismen_Naars.md): compact natural history dossier with 10 field manual-like profiles of Naar's iconic lead organisms.

## Connection to development and reproduction

The basics explain why Enari bodies can be assigned gender from the moment they hatch, but at the same time retain a relatively uniform child shape for a long time. The detailed CV description can be found under [Development and Ontogenesis](../06_Entwicklung_und_Ontogenese/00_Overview.md), the reproductive framework under [Reproduction of the Enari](../03_Fortpflanzung/00_Overview.md).

The planetological and climatic background of the homeworld is additionally documented under [Astronomy and Planetology](../../04_Astronomie/00_Overview.md).
