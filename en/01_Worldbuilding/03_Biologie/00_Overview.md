## Current focus

Baseline profiles for all five Enari sexes are now in place: [Enu](./04_Geschlechter/Enu/00_Overview.md), [Enel](./04_Geschlechter/Enel/00_Overview.md), [Enor](./04_Geschlechter/Enor/00_Overview.md), [Enath](./04_Geschlechter/Enath/00_Overview.md), and [Enis](./04_Geschlechter/Enis/00_Overview.md). These pages collect detailed material on anatomy, reproductive contributions, endocrine specialization, and the link between biological function and social role.

The sections on [phenotypics and clusters](./05_Phaenotypik_und_Cluster/00_Overview.md) and [development and ontogeny](./06_Entwicklung_und_Ontogenese/00_Overview.md) are currently among the most developed. Together they describe how Enari become readable from early brood development through childhood and puberty into old age, without collapsing culture, appearance, and sex into one another.

In addition, [evolution](./01_Grundlagen/02_Evolution.md) offers a coherent hypothesis for the gradual emergence of the five-sex system.
