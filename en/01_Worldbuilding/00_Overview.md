# Enari worldbuilding

This folder collects the core worldbuilding materials for the Enari Project. It combines linguistic development, cultural order, biological principles, astronomical framework data and visual references to form a common working basis.

## Main areas

- `01_Languages`: diachronic and synchronic language development of the Enarian world, including [Proto-Veyatic](./01_Languages/01_Ur-Veyatisch/00_Overview.md), [Old-Veyata](./01_Languages/02_Alt-Veyata/00_Overview.md), [Veyrathi](./01_Languages/03_Veyrathi/00_Overview.md), [Veytharian](./01_Languages/04_Veytharisch/00_Overview.md) and [Saithan](./01_Languages/05_Saithanisch/00_Overview.md).
- `02_Kultur`: social and institutional foundations such as [children's homes](./02_Kultur/01_Kinderhaeuser.md), [caste system](./02_Kultur/02_Kastensystem.md), [education](./02_Kultur/03_Erziehung.md), [military](./02_Kultur/04_Militaer.md), [rituals and everyday culture](./02_Kultur/05_Rituale_und_Alltagskultur.md), the [Veyrath Republic](./02_Kultur/06_Veyrathische_Republik/00_Overview.md) and the [Veyrath Crown](./02_Kultur/07_Veytharische_Krone/00_Overview.md).
- [03_Biology](./03_Biologie/00_Overview.md): biological basics of the Enari with evolution, body structure, reproduction, the profiles of all five sexes, a developed phenotypic system consisting of clusters and markers as well as its own ontogeny from brood development to age.
- `04_Astronomie`: Enari star system, homeworld, climate and space context with [Astronomy and Planetology](./04_Astronomie/00_Overview.md) as an introduction.
- `99_Medien`: Image material, anatomical diagrams, reference views as well as cultural, natural and astronomical archives such as [Cultural areas and nations](./99_Medien/09_Kulturraeume_und_Nationen/00_Overview.md), [Flora](./99_Medien/10_Flora/00_Overview.md), [Fauna](./99_Medien/11_Fauna/00_Overview.md), [Geology and landscape areas](./99_Medien/12_Geologie_und_Landschaftsraeume/00_Overview.md), [Astronomy and Space Travel](./99_Medien/13_Astronomie_und_Raumfahrt/00_Overview.md) and the [Media Naming Scheme](./99_Medien/00_Namensschema.md).

## Current status

Enari biology is currently one of the most developed areas. Clear profiles are available for [Enu](./03_Biologie/04_Geschlechter/Enu/00_Overview.md), [Enel](./03_Biologie/04_Geschlechter/Enel/00_Overview.md), [Enor](./03_Biologie/04_Geschlechter/Enor/00_Overview.md), [Enath](./03_Biologie/04_Geschlechter/Enath/00_Overview.md) and [Enis](./03_Biologie/04_Geschlechter/Enis/00_Overview.md), an [evolutionary Hypothesis](./03_Biologie/01_Grundlagen/02_Evolution.md) for the origin of the five-sex reproductive system, a [marker and cluster system](./03_Biologie/05_Phaenotypik_und_Cluster/00_Overview.md) for visible variation and an [ontogenesis section](./03_Biologie/06_Entwicklung_und_Ontogenese/00_Overview.md) for maturation, life stages and aging.

A new addition is an astronomical-planetological framework with [Naar (Tharun) and the Iyath system](./04_Astronomie/01_Naar_und_das_Iyath-System.md). This means that the star system, moons, climate cycles, bloom phases and the basic space geography of the home system are now documented.

The language area is broad and documents several historical and functional language levels. Culture, astronomy and media form the social, physical and visual context and are gradually being expanded.
