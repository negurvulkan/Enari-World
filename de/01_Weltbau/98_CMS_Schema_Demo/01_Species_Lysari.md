# Lysari

The Lysari are a semi-aquatic intelligent species from the twilight oceans of Astraea.

They are known for luminous skin patterns, high collective memory culture, and a strong tradition of biological record keeping. Their scholars maintain shared research vaults together with the [[03_Institution_Star_Archive.md|Star Archive]].
